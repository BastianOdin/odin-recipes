Onion Relish

Description

The tanginess on the side, to give your dish that little extra

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Bay leaves
  • Juniper berries

Steps

  1. Add water to a pot
  2. Add all ingredients except the onions and bring to a boil
  3. Cut onions an put them into desired containers
  4. Pour liquid over and seal tightly
