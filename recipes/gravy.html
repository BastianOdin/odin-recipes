Gravy

Description

The one sauce to rule them all: gravy. Its essence is what every hearty dish wants. Umami and salty, full of richness, with a deep flavour profile.

Ingredients

  • Root vegetables
  • Red wine
  • Tomato paste
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Sear vegetables golden brown from all sides
  2. Add tomato paste to sear as well
  3. Deglaze with red wine
  4. Add water and herbs
  5. Cook for one to three hours
  6. Strain the sauce into a new pot
  7. Reduce, reduce and reduce
