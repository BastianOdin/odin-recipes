Martino Noodles

Description

The tanginess on the side, to give your dish that little extra

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Canned tomatos
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook spagetti almost al dente
  2. Add all other ingredients to a big pan
  3. Simmer for at least 30 min
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Add spagetti to the pan and cook for another one to three minutes
